FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ERPNext.

What is ERPNext and who is it for?

ERPNext is a free, open-source ERP system designed for small and medium businesses. It covers all aspects of business management including accounting, inventory, CRM, HR, projects, and more. Whether you're a startup or an established company, ERPNext scales to meet your needs.

Is ERPNext really free to use?

Yes! ERPNext is 100% free and open-source under the GNU General Public License. You can download, install, and use it without any licensing fees. You only pay for hosting, implementation, and customization if needed.

Can ERPNext handle multiple companies?

Absolutely! ERPNext supports managing multiple companies within a single installation. Each company can have its own chart of accounts, warehouses, and settings while sharing common master data like suppliers and items.

Is my data secure with ERPNext?

Security is a top priority. ERPNext offers role-based access control, encrypted data storage, secure authentication, and regular security updates. Since you host your own instance, you have complete control over your data.

Can I customize ERPNext for my business needs?

Yes! ERPNext is highly customizable. You can add custom fields, create new doctypes, build custom reports, and modify workflows without touching the core code. For deeper customizations, you can extend the system with custom apps.
